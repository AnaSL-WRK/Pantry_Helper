What you can do

Click to view your permissions.

⌄
{% if role == 'Viewer' %}

You are in read-only mode.

  • You can view pantry items, recipes, and suggested recipes.
  • You cannot manage pantry items, food usage, recipes, or the household.
{% elif role == 'Member' %}

You can help track food usage.

  • You can view pantry items, recipes, and suggested recipes.
  • You can manage food usage by marking items as consumed or wasted.
  • You cannot manage pantry items, recipes, or the household.
{% elif role == 'InventoryManager' %}

You can manage pantry stock, but not the household.

  • You can view pantry items, recipes, and suggested recipes.
  • You can manage pantry items by adding, editing, and deleting food.
  • You can manage food usage by marking items as consumed or wasted.
  • You can add ingredients.
  • You cannot manage household recipes or household members.
{% elif role == 'HouseholdAdmin' %}

You have full household access.

  • You can view pantry items, recipes, and suggested recipes.
  • You can manage pantry items by adding, editing, and deleting food.
  • You can manage food usage by marking items as consumed or wasted.
  • You can add ingredients.
  • You can manage household recipes by creating, editing, and deleting them.
  • You can manage the household by editing details, adding members, changing roles, and removing members.
{% endif %}
{% endif %}
